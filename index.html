NFL Player Compare

Compare one player's stats to another for any particular week of any of the last five seasons. To compare the statistics of two players, first pick a position, a year, and a week, then hit the "Start" button.

Next, choose one player from each menu. Then hit the "Compare" button, and statistics for each player will appear.
